== Description ==

**Your products deserve options that stand out.** 🎨✨

YaySwatches is a powerful WooCommerce variation swatches plugin that transforms boring dropdowns into beautiful color, image, label swatches with various visual effects. Help customers see their choices clearly and pick variations faster. No coding needed.

Customize styles, improve product clarity, and create a smoother shopping experience that converts.

It works seamlessly with WooCommerce, most page builders and block themes. 

👋  [**Quick Demo**](https://demo.yaycommerce.com/yayswatches/) | [**Admin Demo**](https://demo.yaycommerce.com/try-yayswatches/)

YaySwatches brings your WooCommerce product variations to life:

- Enable color, image, and label swatches 
- Zoom preview or enlarged image of swatch
- Display full color/attribute name on hover
- Shows additional info when hovering over a swatch
- Customers can select variations directly from the product listing (Pro version)
- Greyed-out or crossed swatches for out-of-stock items (Pro version)
- Dual-color swatches supported

###⚡️ FEATURES

YaySwatches is a feature-packed **WooCommerce variation swatches** plugin with a modern and simplified user interface. 

It combines WooCommerce global attributes and custom product attributes in a single dashboard. 

Just visit YaySwatches options and settings, you will see all available product attributes and set the styles you like.

**Color Swatches**
Displaying the corresponding product image swatches based on colors.

**Automated Variation Image Swatches**
If your products come with different variant images, they will be shown automatically. 

**Customizable Swatch Bubbles**
Plenty of design options, including swatch shape, size, border, color, spacing, and tooltip.

**Customizable Swatch Button**
You can tweak the variation button design to fit your website's color profiles. Even better, you can display buttons for attributes like size, material, model, etc. right on the shop/category page.

**Powerful Attribute Options**
YaySwatches works with pre-existing attributes (free version) and also custom product attributes (premium version only).

**Enhanced Shop Page & Catalogs (PRO)**
Shop page, catalogs, and category pages are fully supported in [YaySwatches Pro](https://yaycommerce.com/yayswatches-variation-swatches-for-woocommerce/) version. You can enable and set a limit to the number of swatch bubbles shown for each product on the shop page. This helps keep your product grid neat and tidy. Once the customer clicks on the rest of the swatch options, they can view the remaining swatches or jump to the product page.

**Out-of-stock Variation Visibility (PRO)**
Show or hide those variations that have been sold out on the product page, shop page, and categories page. You can choose a predesigned style for showing/hiding out-of-stock variants to fit your WooCommerce store.

**Making Your Products Easy to Find**
The ultimate goal of this plugin is to make browsing your products or services extremely easy!

== Installation ==
Setup with Yay Swatches is easy and takes less than one minute.

1. Upload the plugin zip file to WordPress Plugins menu.
2. Activate the plugin through the ‘Plugins‘ menu in WordPress.
3. Go to YayCommerce > YaySwatches to start configuring WooCommerce variation swatches.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Is YaySwatches plugin compatible with any WooCommerce themes? =
Yes, it goes along well with all popular WooCommerce themes. In some cases, it may require additional tweaks to make it work seamlessly with your current theme.

= How to use color swatches for those custom product attributes that are not added to "Attribute" menu?=
This feature is available in [YaySwatches Pro](https://yaycommerce.com/yayswatches-variation-swatches-for-woocommerce/). You can go to WooCommerce admin > Products > Edit product and look for **YaySwatches Settings**, there you can find the current custom product attribute terms and design their variant selector types.

= I got issues. How can I get support? =
Please [create a topic](https://wordpress.org/support/plugin/yayswatches) or [contact us](https://yaycommerce.com/support/) to get help.
Please provide screenshots of the active WooCommerce plugins on your site to expedite the troubleshooting process, as there may be a conflict with a third-party plugin.

= Is YaySwatches GDPR-compliant? =
Yes, YaySwatches is DSGVO- and GDPR-compliant. The plugin does not collect any sensitive personal data, so you can be confident that your customer information is safe and private.
